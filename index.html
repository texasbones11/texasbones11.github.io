Our Story

Will and Kate started dating while Kate was working for Austin Equine Hospital in Driftwood, TX. 2019 was filled with new love and adventure with trips to the Kentucky derby, Florida, Arizona, and New Mexico. While we thought our story would continue in TX, we learned quickly that we aren’t in control. In 2020 we realized that family is the most important aspect of life. We have since moved back to the Ranch in New Mexico. Will continues to work for the same company remotely... very, very remotely. Will has seamlessly taken on the role of “Uncle Will” and we very much look forward to making that official May 1st 2021. While it has been difficult to plan a wedding during the pandemic, we can promise we will have the cutest two flower girls available. We are grateful for the love and support of our family and friends and very much look forward to this new chapter.
